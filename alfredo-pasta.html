Alfredo Pasta

Best tasing italian dish I can make!

Recipe consists of a base of whole wheat rotoni. Alfredo sauce that is fresh. Broccoli added with red peppers at the end.

Ingredients
  • Whole wheat pasta
  • Alfredo pasta
  • Broccoli
  • Red pepper flakes
Recipe
  1. Boil water on high
  2. Once water is boiling, add pasta to pot
  3. Boil pasta on high for 9 minutes
  4. Strain pasta
  5. Add alfredo to pot and stir
  6. Add brocolli and red flakes and stir
  7. Serve and enjoy!
